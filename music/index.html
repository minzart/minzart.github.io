Music

Right now, I am studying piano full-time at the Schulich School of Music (McGill University). I halted almost every aspect of my life except for piano, but I am slowly trying to find some middle ground. Recently, I started a short blog series documenting some of the things I learned from my foray into music.

I will try to post things related to music in some way.
